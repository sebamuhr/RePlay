Topics — 👍 fill your feeds · 👎 hide from RePlay

Like a topic to fill Home & Shorts. Dislike any word, topic or name (e.g. football, Trump) to hide every video that mentions it — title, description, tags or channel. A term can only be in one list.

Channels — 👍 follow · 🚫 hide

While watching, use 👍 Follow channel or 🚫 Hide channel. The LED dot up top turns 🔴 when a followed channel has new uploads — tap it to load them (cheap: ~1 quota unit per channel).

Auto-fill — keep your feed fresh, cheaply

When you open RePlay, pull new uploads from your followed + auto-tracked channels (collected from your searches). This is the cheap path: ~1 unit per channel, not 100 per topic.
