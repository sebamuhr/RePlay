Auto-fill feed — keep your home feed fresh

When the newest video in your feed is more than 1 hour old, RePlay pulls the latest videos for your 👍 liked topics (a real YouTube search) plus new uploads from your followed channels into your feed. This happens when you open the app and whenever you browse the home feed — never silently in the background. If nothing new turns up, it waits another hour before trying again. Videos are kept for the retention window below, then drop off.

Only search for videos in these languages — your feed stays the same wherever you travel (no location/IP detection). Add as many as you like. Leave empty for no language filter.

Pool (home feed)

Your home feed (no search active) is a local cache of videos. Searching no longer adds to it, but if it got filled with junk, empty it here.
